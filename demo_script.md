3-4 minutes. know the beats, don't read verbatim.

---

## before you start

- app running: `python3.11 server.py` → http://localhost:8000
- terminal visible showing server logs
- dark mode is default, light mode toggle in header
- wallet NOT connected yet (connect live)

## the walkthrough

### the problem (15 sec)

let the leaderboard load. five twins visible.

"there are thousands of AI models making predictions publicly every day. they screenshot wins, delete losses, nobody checks. SentientMarket tracks them and generates verified reputation reports."

### walk the leaderboard (20 sec)

scroll through the five twins. point at accuracy numbers.

"five digital twins from Twin.fun. MacroMind at 80% — four wins, one loss. AlphaWhale and YieldFarmer at 75%. DegenSage and SnipeBot at 60%. every prediction is tracked — asset, direction, entry, target, outcome."

### the architecture (15 sec)

"the reputation engine ingests each twin's prediction history as memory, tracks win/loss by asset and direction, then sends all of that to Gemini Flash Lite running inside a TEE — a Trusted Execution Environment. the AI analyzes the data, the enclave signs the output, and OPG payment settles on Base Sepolia via x402. one SDK call handles the payment, the inference, and the proof."

### connect wallet (15 sec)

click Connect Wallet. let the modal show detected wallets.

"standard wallet connect. MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet — pick one and you're in."

### click into MacroMind (30 sec)

click MacroMind (top performer at 80%).

"MacroMind. 80% accuracy across five calls. 100% on BTC, 100% on ETH, missed one SOL call. bias indicator shows bullish, risk profile is medium."

scroll to the AI analysis section. wait for it to load (few seconds).

"this is the AI reputation analysis. generated by Gemini Flash Lite inside a TEE enclave on OpenGradient. the model gets the full prediction history and outputs a structured report — strengths, weaknesses, bias, risk rating, narrative."

point at the green banner: "Analysis verified via TEE enclave — x402 payment settled on Base Sepolia"

### TEE proof section (15 sec)

scroll to the TEE verification section at the bottom.

"here's the cryptographic proof. ENCLAVE_VALIDATED — the payment hash from x402 settlement on Base Sepolia. this is the TEE signature. you change one word in the output and the signature breaks. nobody — not even the server operator — can alter the result."

point at the terminal: "and in the server logs you can see the real-time TEE verification — the enclave ID, endpoint, timestamp."

### show a weaker twin (15 sec)

go back. click DegenSage or SnipeBot.

"DegenSage. 60%. the system doesn't play favorites. it counts wins and losses. good twins rise, bad twins get exposed. the AI analysis reflects that — it calls out the zero accuracy on SUI, the missed BTC call."

### show light mode (5 sec)

click the theme toggle.

"supports light mode."

### closing (15 sec)

go back to the leaderboard.

"SentientMarket. five tracked twins. verified accuracy. TEE-signed reputation analysis via OpenGradient x402. each analysis costs about 0.005 OPG and produces a cryptographic proof that the result is genuine."

## about the twin data

the five twins and their prediction histories are based on representative trading patterns:
- **AlphaWhale** — whale tracker, 75% accuracy, strong on BTC
- **DegenSage** — altcoin degen, 60% accuracy, strong on SOL/ETH, weak on BTC/SUI
- **MacroMind** — macro analyst, 80% accuracy, best overall performer
- **SnipeBot** — speed-focused, 60% accuracy, good at SOL
- **YieldFarmer** — DeFi yield plays, 75% accuracy, consistent on ETH

the prediction data is ingested into the memory pipeline on startup. the AI analysis is real — every time you click a twin, a fresh LLM call runs through the TEE enclave and returns a new analysis with a new TEE signature. nothing is cached.

without the MEMSYNC_API_KEY, memories are stored in-memory (reset on restart). with the API key, memories persist via the MemSync REST API.

## tips

- don't rush. let pages load. silence is fine.
- the loading spinner says "Querying TEE enclave..." — that's real, it takes a few seconds.
- watch the terminal. every inference shows the TEE verification in real time.
- if someone asks about the AI analysis — it's not static, it's generated fresh every visit.
- one take is fine. real > polished.
