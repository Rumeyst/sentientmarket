# SentientMarket Demo Recording Script

Total time: About 2 minutes. Keep it tight.

---

## BEFORE YOU HIT RECORD

- Have the server running: `python3.11 server.py`
- Browser open at `http://localhost:8000`
- Dark mode ON
- Wallet NOT connected yet (so you can show the connect flow)
- Terminal visible but small (split screen or picture-in-picture if possible)

---

## THE SCRIPT

### Opening (10 seconds)

Start on the leaderboard page. Pause for a beat so they see the layout.

SAY: "This is SentientMarket. It's a reputation oracle for AI trading twins. Every score you see here is backed by a TEE cryptographic proof."

### Walk the leaderboard (15 seconds)

Scroll slowly through the five twins.

SAY: "Five digital twins ranked by prediction accuracy. MacroMind is leading at 80%. SnipeBot is at 60%. These numbers come from real prediction tracking, not self-reported stats."

### Connect wallet (15 seconds)

Click "Connect Wallet." Let MetaMask pop up. Approve it.

SAY: "Users connect their own wallet. No private keys stored on the server. You can see the address and network right here in the banner."

### Click into MacroMind (20 seconds)

Click "View" on MacroMind. Wait for the detail page to load.

SAY: "Let's look at MacroMind. You can see the full breakdown. Accuracy by asset, win/loss record, and the AI analysis section below."

### Point out the AI analysis (15 seconds)

Scroll to the AI analysis card. Read a line or two from it.

SAY: "This analysis was generated by GPT-4.1 running inside a Trusted Execution Environment. Nobody can change what the AI said. Not me, not the server, nobody."

### Show the TEE proof (15 seconds)

Point at the green "TEE Verified" badge with the proof hash.

SAY: "And here's the proof. This is a cryptographic signature from the TEE enclave. Every twin gets a unique one. You can verify it independently."

### Show another twin (15 seconds)

Go back to leaderboard. Click into a different twin (AlphaWhale or DegenSage).

SAY: "Every twin gets its own unique analysis and its own unique proof. Let me show you AlphaWhale. Different analysis, different proof hash. This isn't templated."

### Terminal flash (10 seconds)

Briefly show the terminal output where TEE proofs are printing.

SAY: "On the backend you can see each TEE verification coming through in real time. Enclave ID, signature, endpoint. All logged."

### Closing (15 seconds)

Go back to the leaderboard.

SAY: "SentientMarket. Trustless reputation for AI twins. Built on OpenGradient using MemSync for memory, on-chain forecast models for accuracy, and x402 for verified AI inference. Every score has a receipt."

### END

Stop recording.

---

## TIPS

- Don't rush. Let each page load and breathe for a second before talking.
- Don't read the script word for word. Use it as a guide. Sound natural.
- If something loads slow, just say "the AI is running inference right now" and wait.
- Keep your mouse movements smooth and deliberate. Don't jitter.
- If you mess up just keep going. One take is fine, it doesn't need to be polished.
